# Portal Overview

The PocketSOC Portal is a web-based dashboard at [portal.pocketsoc.com](https://portal.pocketsoc.com) where administrators manage their organization, team, vendor configurations, and billing.

## What you can do in the portal

- **Organization settings** — Set your organization name and billing email
- **Team management** — Invite members, assign admin/member roles, remove users
- **Groups** — Organize team members into groups and assign vendor profiles to each group
- **Devices** — View registered devices, deactivate lost or unused devices
- **Vendor configurations** — Add, edit, and remove CrowdStrike or Defender API credentials
- **Billing** — Upgrade your plan, switch between monthly and annual billing, manage payment methods
- **Audit log** — Review all administrative actions taken in your organization

## Roles

PocketSOC has two portal roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage team, groups, devices, vendor configs, billing, and settings |
| **Member** | View-only in the portal. Can use the iOS app to view detections assigned to their groups |

The first user who creates an organization is automatically an **Admin**. Additional users join via invitation.

## Navigation

The portal sidebar includes:

- **Devices** — Registered iOS devices
- **Team** — Organization members and invitations
- **Groups** — User groups and profile assignments
- **Audit Log** — Admin-only activity log
- **Settings** — Organization info, vendor configs, billing
